About the Firm

Client-Focused Legal
Practice in Thrissur

V and R Law Associates is a Wadakkanchery-based legal practice led by Advocate Riju Raj and Advocate Vishnudev. The firm provides client-focused legal services, mainly handling criminal matters, bail applications, and general litigation, with an emphasis on clear guidance and timely case handling.

With a commitment to structured legal reasoning and effective courtroom response, the firm has built a reputation for handling urgent, time-sensitive cases with the precision they demand. Every client receives clear communication and strategic counsel from the very first consultation.
